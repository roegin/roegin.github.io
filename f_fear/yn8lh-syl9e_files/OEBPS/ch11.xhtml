Feel the Fear and Do It Anyway®_【彩云小译】

Susan Jeffers
previous page next page

I have talked about a lot of powerful tools: Affirmations, Saying Yes to Your Universe, Positive Thinking, Taking Responsibility, No-Lose Decisions, Choosing Love and Trust, Learning to Give, and more. The reason these are powerful tools is that they open us to a place within, which, when tapped, allows us to feel “full-filled.”
我已经谈到了很多强大的工具: 肯定，对你的宇宙说是，积极的思考，承担责任，不失为决定，选择爱和信任，学习给予，等等。这些工具之所以强大，是因为它们打开了我们内在的一个空间，当我们开启这个空间时，会让我们感到“充实”

This place within has been given a multitude of names, some of which are: Higher Self, Inner Self, Superconscious, Higher Conscious, and God-self. I like the term “Higher Self,” simply because it implies that we can move above that part of us that dwells on the petty things that cause fear, hate, scarcity, and all other forms of negativity. It brings to mind a new plane of existence that has little to do with everyday upsets and struggle.
内在的这个地方被赋予了许多名字，其中一些是: 更高的自我、内在的自我、超意识、更高的意识和上帝的自我。我喜欢“更高的自我”这个术语，仅仅是因为它暗示我们可以超越那部分我们自己，那部分我们可以停留在那些引起恐惧、憎恨、匮乏和所有其他形式的消极的小事上。它让我们想起了一个与日常烦恼和挣扎无关的新的存在层面。

There is a body of psychologists who believe in the existence of the Higher Self and the influence it can exert upon the individual. Some refer to their work with the Higher Self as “Height Psychology.” Others refer to it as “Transpersonal Psychology.” There are also many educators and metaphysicians whose work encompasses the realm of the Higher Self.
有一群心理学家相信更高自我的存在及其对个体的影响。一些人将他们与更高自我的工作称为“高度心理学”其他人称之为“超个人心理学”还有许多教育家和形而上学家的工作包括更高自我的领域。

They have proposed that this Higher Self is capable of a high degree of sensitivity and attunement to a harmonious flow within the universe. It is the container of many sublime virtues—creativity, intuition, trust, love, joy, inspiration, aspiration, caring, giving—everything we, in our heart of hearts, would like to experience.
他们已经提出，这个更高的自我能够对宇宙中的和谐流动有高度的敏感性和协调性。它是许多崇高美德的容器——创造力、直觉、信任、爱、快乐、灵感、渴望、关怀、奉献——我们内心深处想要体验的一切。

Too many of us seem to be searching for something “out there” to make our lives complete. We feel alienated, lonely, and empty. No matter what we do or have, we never feel full-filled. This feeling of emptiness or intense loneliness is our clue that we are off-course, and that we need to correct our direction. Often we think that the correction lies in a new mate, house, car, job, or whatever. Not so.
我们中有太多人似乎在寻找某种“外在”的东西，以使我们的生活变得完整。我们感到疏远、孤独和空虚。无论我们做什么或拥有什么，我们都不会感到满足。这种空虚感或强烈的孤独感是我们偏离轨道的线索，我们需要纠正我们的方向。我们常常认为，纠正在于一个新的伴侣，房子，汽车，工作，或者其他什么。事实并非如此。

I believe that what all of us are really searching for is this divine essence within ourselves. When we are far from our Higher Self, we feel what Roberto Assagioli has so aptly called “Divine Homesickness.” When you are feeling this sense of being lost, or off course, the thing to do to find your way home again is simply to use the tools that will align you with your Higher Self—and thus allow the good feelings to flow once again.
我相信我们所有人真正在寻找的是我们内在的神圣本质。当我们远离更高的自我时，我们会感受到罗伯特 · 阿萨吉奥利所说的“神圣的乡愁”当你感到迷失或偏离航向时，重新找到回家之路的方法就是简单地使用工具，让你与更高的自我保持一致，从而让美好的感觉再次流动起来。

You might ask, “Where has this Higher Self been hiding all my life?” We often hear the expression “body, mind, and spirit.” It is used to define our whole being. Modern society has been primarily concerned with body and mind. The spirit part, which encompasses the Higher Self, has somehow gotten lost in the shuffle. As yet, there are relatively few places that teach anything about the Higher Self. So it is not surprising that we have focused almost totally on the intellectual and physical parts of ourselves. In fact, many of us haven’t even been aware that we have a spiritual part.
你可能会问: “这个更高的自我一生都藏在哪里?”我们经常听到这样的说法“身体，思想和精神。”它被用来定义我们的整个存在。现代社会主要关注的是身体和精神。包含更高自我的精神部分，不知何故在混乱中迷失了。到目前为止，只有相对较少的地方教授关于更高自我的任何东西。所以我们几乎完全关注自己的智力和身体部分也就不足为奇了。事实上，我们中的许多人甚至没有意识到我们有一个精神的部分。

Add to that the fact that many people don’t even like the word “spiritual.” They tune out the minute it is mentioned. The reason is that they confuse “spiritual” with religion and God. For those who are not religious, the word “spiritual” is a turnoff.
此外，许多人甚至不喜欢“精神”这个词一提到这个词，他们就不理会了。原因是他们混淆了“精神”与宗教和上帝。对于那些没有宗教信仰的人来说，“精神”这个词是一个令人反感的词。

The way I use this word will be acceptable to you whether you are religious or not. When I speak of the spiritual, I speak of the Higher Self, the place within that is loving, kind, abundant, joyful, and all those other qualities I mentioned earlier. Believe me when I tell you that unless you consciously or unconsciously tap into that spiritual part within, you will experience perpetual discontent.
无论你是否信教，我使用这个词的方式都会被你所接受。当我谈到灵性的时候，我说的是更高的自我，那里是充满爱、善良、丰富、快乐的地方，以及我前面提到的所有其他品质。相信我，当我告诉你，除非你有意识或无意识地挖掘内在的精神部分，否则你将经历永恒的不满足。

I am sure that every one of you has at times operated from the spiritual part of yourself without labeling it as that. Have you ever given something to someone and felt so good it brought tears to your eyes? Have you ever been so overwhelmed with the beauty of something, a sunset or a flower, that you were filled with a sense of abundance? Have you ever looked beyond someone’s ugly behavior and felt only love for them as you saw their pain? Have you ever cried tears of joy in a movie when a character overcame a severe obstacle? If you did any of these things, it could be said you were operating from the Higher Self. You transcended the world of the petty— “She didn’t even say thank you”; “He never picks up his dirty socks”; “Why doesn’t he call?”—and touched a world of beauty beyond.
我确信你们每一个人都曾经有时候从自己的精神部分运作，而不把它贴上那样的标签。你是否曾经给过某人某样东西，感觉如此美好，以至于让你流下了眼泪？你是否曾经被一些美丽的东西，比如日落或者花朵所淹没，以至于你充满了富足的感觉？你有没有看到过别人丑陋的行为，当你看到他们的痛苦时，你只是爱他们？你是否曾经在一部电影中，当一个角色克服了一个严重的障碍，你会喜极而泣？如果你做了这些事情中的任何一件，可以说你是从更高的自我出发。你超越了小气的世界——“她甚至没有说声谢谢”; “他从来不收拾他的脏袜子”; “他为什么不打电话?”ーー触摸到了一个美丽的世界。

You experience an incredible high when your Personal Higher Self hooks into a Group Higher Self. If your heart swelled at the closing ceremonies of the Olympics—when you got a sense of how magnificent this world would be if we all acted as one for the good of all—you got a sense of the Group Higher Self. The power and love that can generate are phenomenal.
当你的个人更高的自我与群体更高的自我挂钩时，你会体验到一种难以置信的高潮。如果你的心在奥运会闭幕式上膨胀起来——当你感觉到如果我们都为了所有人的利益而团结一致，这个世界将是多么的壮丽——你就会感觉到一群更高的自我。能够产生的力量和爱是非凡的。

As you are undoubtedly aware, power, and even a sense of a high, can be generated by evil as well. The difference is the almost blissful feeling you get from all things generated by the Higher Self, which is inherently a loving space. Power from a negative place does not alleviate our Divine Homesickness. In fact, it takes us far, far from home. Thus, when the temporary sense of power leaves, you are left lost and lonely and frightened. When you operate from the Higher Self, you feel centered and abundant—in fact, overflowing. When you experience this abundance, your fears automatically disappear.
正如你们毫无疑问地意识到的那样，权力，甚至是一种崇高的感觉，也可以由邪恶产生。区别在于你从更高的自我所产生的所有事物中获得的几乎是幸福的感觉，而更高的自我本质上是一个充满爱的空间。来自消极地方的力量并不能减轻我们神圣的思乡之情。事实上，它让我们远离家乡。因此，当暂时的权力感消失时，你会感到迷失、孤独和恐惧。当你从更高的自我出发运作时，你会感到中心和充实——事实上，充实。当你体验到这种富足时，你的恐惧会自动消失。

The Higher Self is also in operation when you create “miracles” in your life—the power to lift a car if a loved one is trapped underneath or the power to accomplish a monumental task that everyone has told you is impossible. I have often heard people say, “I don’t know how I did it, but I did!” Their power came from their Higher Self.
当你在生活中创造“奇迹”的时候，更高的自我也在运转——如果你所爱的人被困在下面，你有能力举起一辆汽车，或者有能力完成每个人都告诉你不可能完成的巨大任务。我经常听到人们说，“我不知道我是怎么做到的，但是我做到了!”他们的力量来自更高的自我。

Now that I’ve introduced you to your Higher Self, let me show you an ultra simple model of being. The model on the following page is not comprehensive; it leaves out many parts of our internal and external worlds. It does serve, however, to remind us that we can choose our experiences of life.
现在我已经向你们介绍了更高的自我，让我向你们展示一个超简单的存在模型。下面这个模型并不全面，它遗漏了我们内在和外在世界的许多部分。然而，它确实提醒我们，我们可以选择我们的生活经历。

The Chatterbox, as you’ll remember, is the part of us that tries to drive us crazy. It is the repository of all our negative input, from the time we were born to the present moment. It contains our childlike ego, which needs constant attention and doesn’t know how to give. The Conscious Mind sends orders to the Subconscious Mind based on the information it gets from either the Higher Self or the Chatterbox. We can train it to choose from either.
正如你所记得的，喋喋不休是我们试图让我们发疯的一部分。它是我们所有负面信息的储存库，从我们出生到现在。它包含了我们孩子般的自我，需要不断的关注，却不知道如何给予。有意识的大脑根据从更高的自我或者话匣子里得到的信息向潜意识发出命令。我们可以训练它从中做出选择。

The Subconscious Mind is a storehouse of a huge amount of information. It also has access to the Universal Energy. It operates in a computer-like fashion, sorting and finding information. For example, you can’t remember a name, and suddenly, when you are least expecting it, it pops up out of nowhere. Your Subconscious Mind has been at work. The Subconscious Mind takes its orders from the Conscious Mind. It does not question or judge. It does not know right from wrong or healthy from unhealthy. Remember the arm experiment in Chapter 5. When the Subconscious Mind was told, “I am a strong and worthy person,” the arm was strengthened. When it was told, “I am a weak and unworthy person,” the arm was easily pushed down. The Subconscious Mind believes what the Conscious Mind tells it whether or not it is true, or even whether you believe it or not.
潜意识是一个巨大的信息仓库。它也可以接触到宇宙的能量。它以类似计算机的方式运作，对信息进行分类和查找。例如，你不记得一个名字，突然，当你最不期待它的时候，它突然出现了。你的潜意识一直在工作。潜意识接受意识的指令。它不会质疑或评判。它不知道对与错，健康与不健康。还记得第五章中的手臂实验吗。当潜意识被告知“我是一个坚强而有价值的人”时，手臂得到了加强。当它被告知“我是一个软弱和不值得的人”时，这只手臂很容易就被压下去了。潜意识相信意识告诉它的，不管它是真是假，甚至不管你是否相信它。

As the model suggests, your mind can choose to listen to your Chatterbox blabbering away with all kinds of self-defeating negativity, or it can choose to listen to your Higher Self, which is self-affirming, loving, giving, and abundant. All of the exercises and concepts suggested in this book are geared toward having the Conscious Mind open its door to the abundance of the Higher Self as opposed to the scarcity of the Chatterbox.
正如这个模型所建议的，你的大脑可以选择倾听你喋喋不休的各种自我挫败的消极言论，或者它可以选择倾听你的更高的自我，这是自我肯定的、爱的、给予的和丰富的。本书中提出的所有练习和概念都是为了让有意识的心灵打开通向更高自我丰富的大门，而不是喋喋不休的缺乏。

The Conscious Mind is often not aware that it is being run by the Chatterbox. And even if there is awareness, the Conscious Mind is so used to listening to the Chatterbox that, in the course of everyday events, it “forgets” to listen to the Higher Self and thus needs constant reminding. This is where the affirmations, positive thinking, tapes, books, sayings, and whatever other tools you have come in handy—to remind your Conscious Mind that you do not have to listen to the Chatterbox any longer.
有意识的头脑通常不知道它是由话匣子控制的。即使有意识，有意识的心灵也是如此习惯于倾听话匣子，以至于在日常事件的过程中，它“忘记”倾听更高的自我，因此需要不断的提醒。这就是肯定、积极思考、磁带、书籍、谚语以及任何其他你能用得上的工具的地方——来提醒你的意识头脑，你不必再听喋喋不休的人说话了。

As with the Higher Self, the Chatterbox has always been there, and will always be there. No use lamenting the fact that it pops up every once in a while! I guarantee that it will. You must realize that you do have a Chatterbox within; and you have more, including the Higher Self. Neither one is right or wrong. Each simply gives you a different experience of life.
就像更高的自我一样，喋喋不休的话匣子一直在那里，而且永远都会在那里。每隔一段时间，它就会出现一次，哀叹这个事实是没有用的！我保证会的。你必须意识到你内心确实有一个话匣子，而且你还有更多，包括更高的自我。没有一个是正确的或错误的。每一种都只是给你一种不同的生活体验。

If you listen to the Chatterbox, your experience of life is fear-producing, and you stop yourself from expanding. If you listen to the Higher Self, your experience of life is joyful and abundant and devoid of fear. You, like everyone else, are an expert at listening to your Chatterbox. Your task is now to become an expert at listening to your Higher Self. Then true choice will be possible.
如果你听喋喋不休的人说话，你的生活经历就会产生恐惧，你就会阻止自己扩张。如果你倾听更高的自我，你的生活体验是快乐的，丰富的，没有恐惧。你，像其他人一样，是一个倾听自己话匣子的专家。你现在的任务是成为倾听更高自我的专家。然后，真正的选择将成为可能。

Why do so many positive things happen when the Conscious Mind chooses to operate from the Higher Self, and why does the reverse happen when it listens to the Chatterbox? It seems that when the Subconscious Mind receives its orders from the Conscious Mind, it seeks to carry them out by connecting internally to the body, feelings, and intellect. Thus, when it hears “I am a weak and unworthy person,” it connects to your body and makes you physically weak. It connects to your feelings and makes you depressed and helpless. It connects to your intellect and makes you think stupidly. It also fills you with negative energy. When it hears “I am a strong and worthy person,” it connects to your body and makes you strong. It connects to your feelings and makes you feel confident and alive. It connects to your intellect and makes you think clearly. It also fills you with positive energy.
为什么这么多积极的事情发生时，有意识的心灵选择从更高的自我操作，为什么相反的事情发生时，它听喋喋不休？看起来，当潜意识接受来自意识的指令时，它试图通过与身体、感觉和智力的内在联系来执行这些指令。因此，当它听到“我是一个软弱和不值得的人”，它连接到你的身体，使你身体虚弱。它联系到你的感受，让你沮丧和无助。它与你的智力联系在一起，让你愚蠢地思考。它也会让你充满负能量。当它听到“我是一个坚强和有价值的人”，它连接到你的身体，使你强大。它与你的感受相连，让你感到自信和活力。它与你的智力联系在一起，使你思维清晰。它也让你充满了积极的能量。

What is more, outside yourself is a Universal Energy without which the world would not exist. In carrying out its orders from the Conscious Mind, the energy of the Subconscious Mind connects to this Universal Energy, which manages to bring back exactly what you have “asked” for. If you put out “I am a weak and unworthy person,” the universe obliges the Subconscious Mind and delivers all sorts of negative things. People step on you. You never achieve anything you want. Everything stands in your way, and you feel powerless to move anything aside.
更重要的是，在你之外是一种宇宙能量，没有它，世界就不会存在。在执行来自意识头脑的命令时，潜意识头脑的能量连接到这个宇宙能量，这个宇宙能量设法把你“要求”的东西带回来。如果你说出“我是一个软弱和不值得的人”，宇宙就会满足潜意识的需求，传递各种负面的东西。人们踩在你身上。你永远不会得到你想要的东西。一切都挡在你的路上，你感到无力把任何东西移到一边。

When you put out “I am a strong and worthy person,” the universe obliges the Subconscious Mind and delivers all sorts of positive things. People respect your strength and treat you fairly. You attain all sorts of wonderful things. Nothing stands in your way as you find the means to remove any obstacles. The key here is that just as your Subconscious Mind does not judge, neither does the Universal Energy.
当你说出“我是一个坚强而有价值的人”时，宇宙就会满足你潜意识的需求，传递出各种积极的东西。人们尊重你的力量，公平地对待你。你获得了各种各样美好的东西。当你找到消除障碍的方法时，没有什么能阻挡你。这里的关键是，正如你的潜意识不会判断，宇宙能量也不会。

Metaphysicians talk about Laws of Universal Energy. One of these is the Law of Attraction. You will recognize it as “like attracts like.” When you send out negative energy, what will you attract? Negative energy. When you send out positive energy, what will you attract? Positive energy. Perhaps this makes clearer why it is imperative to train your mind to send out only positive thoughts.
形而上学家谈论宇宙能量定律。其中之一就是吸引力法则(新思维)。你会认为它是“相似的吸引相似的”当你发出负能量时，你会吸引什么？负能量。当你发出正能量时，你会吸引什么？积极的能量。也许这更清楚地说明了为什么训练你的大脑只发出积极的想法是必要的。

Some of you might have trouble accepting the idea of Universal Energy. You don’t need to believe this concept in order to tap into your Higher Self. However, when you can see yourself connected to something bigger than yourself, you no longer feel you must do it all alone. Your sense of power becomes highly magnified, and your fears are greatly diminished. This relates back to the Level 3 Fear: “I’m afraid I can’t handle it.” Now you can see that with the Universal Energy on your side, you can learn to trust not only yourself, but the universe as well. This kind of dual trust ultimately signals the end of fear.
你们中的一些人可能难以接受宇宙能量的概念。你不需要为了进入更高的自我而相信这个概念。然而，当你看到自己与比自己更大的事物联系在一起时，你就不再觉得你必须独自完成它。你的权力感会被高度放大，你的恐惧也会大大减少。这与第三级恐惧有关: “我恐怕我无法处理它。”现在你可以看到，有了宇宙能量站在你这边，你不仅可以学会相信自己，也可以相信整个宇宙。这种双重信任最终标志着恐惧的结束。

One of the tools the Subconscious Mind uses to connect you with what you are looking for is your intuition. Those strange messages you get are the Subconscious Mind saying, “I found it!” Anyone who has experienced the power of intuition cannot deny that something is acting on our behalf if we listen to it. By learning to trust your intuition, “miracles” seem to happen.
潜意识用来将你和你所寻找的东西联系起来的工具之一就是你的直觉。你得到的那些奇怪的信息就是潜意识在说: “我找到了!”任何经历过直觉力量的人都不能否认，如果我们倾听它，它就会代表我们行动。通过学会相信自己的直觉，“奇迹”似乎就会发生。

Our intuition is always operating for us—usually, however, we don’t act on it. When I made the conscious decision to start acting on thoughts that were coming through my head, amazing “coincidences” began to occur. My original “fear class” came about as a result of my intuition. At the time, I had a vague notion that one day in the future I wanted to teach a course on fear. I put it off indefinitely, largely because I was too busy with other things to write up the course description and outline and then find a school that would want me to teach such a course. It seemed like a lot of work.
我们的直觉总是在为我们操作——然而，通常情况下，我们不会对它采取行动。当我做出有意识的决定，开始按照脑海中闪过的想法行动时，惊人的“巧合”开始出现。我最初的“恐惧课”来自于我的直觉。当时，我有一个模糊的概念，在未来的某一天，我想教一门关于恐惧的课程。我无限期地推迟了，很大程度上是因为我忙于其他事情，没有时间写课程描述和大纲，也没有时间去找一个愿意让我教这门课的学校。这看起来像是一项繁重的工作。

One day, as I sat at my desk working, a strong message came to mind. It said, “Go to the New School.” I couldn’t figure out why this message came into my head. I had never attended the New School for Social Research. I knew no one there. In fact, I didn’t even know where it was. Out of curiosity, I decided to go. I told my secretary I was going to the New School, and she asked why. I said, “I don’t know!” She looked at me strangely as I walked out the door.

Assagioli is the founder of psychosynthesis, an amazing model of self-integration and self-realization. Psychosynthesis employs many techniques that are valuable in freeing us from past conditioning, resolving internal conflicts (such as, “I want to be taken care of” versus “I want to be totally independent”) and awakening our loving and creative powers.
我也鼓励你们学习荣格和阿萨吉奥利的思想，他们都是超个人心理学领域的伟大思想家。Assagioli 是心理综合的创始人，心理综合是一个了不起的自我整合和了悟真我的模型。心理综合运用了许多有价值的技术，帮助我们从过去的条件反射中解脱出来，解决内心的冲突(比如，“我想被照顾”和“我想完全独立”) ，唤醒我们的爱和创造力。

One of the powerful tools used by the practitioners of psychosynthesis and other disciplines is the guided visualization. This can be one of the most effective ways of quickly touching the Higher Self. There are a number of wonderful books written on the subject, and again I suggest you read them. But to really know the power of a guided visualization, you must experience one. Therefore, I encourage you to take a guided visualization workshop or buy audios that include one. Reading gives you an understanding, but going through one gives you the experience—a dramatic difference indeed.
心理综合和其他学科的实践者使用的一个强有力的工具是引导性的可视化。这可能是快速接触更高自我最有效的方法之一。关于这个主题有很多精彩的书籍，我再次建议你阅读它们。但是要真正了解引导性形象化的力量，你必须亲身体验一下。因此，我鼓励你参加一个引导性的可视化工作坊，或者购买一些包含可视化工作坊的音频。阅读可以给你一种理解，但是通过阅读可以给你一种体验——确实是一种巨大的不同。

In brief, a guided visualization requires that you close your eyes, relax your body, and listen to the person giving you instructions. It uses the power of your imagination to see life as it would be seen if you listen only to your Higher Self. If you are fearful, your imagination is plugged into the Chatterbox, and often the only things you can conjure up are horror stories. With guided visualizations you can learn to push aside the Chatterbox for a moment and experience feelings and see mental pictures the likes of which you have never felt or seen before. The pictures are often of such beauty that you will be moved to tears of joy. Even negative pictures that arise are valuable; they often reveal insights you have been hiding from yourself.
简而言之，一个引导性的可视化需要你闭上眼睛，放松身体，并且倾听给你指示的人。它利用你想象力的力量来看待生活，就像你只听从更高的自我所看到的那样。如果你感到恐惧，你的想象力就会被塞进喋喋不休的话匣子里，通常你能想象出来的只有恐怖故事。通过引导可视化，你可以学会暂时抛开喋喋不休的话题，体验感受，看到你以前从未感受过或看到过的心理画面。这些图片往往是如此的美丽，以至于你会感动得喜极而泣。即使是负面的照片也是有价值的，它们常常揭示了你一直在隐藏的真知灼见。

There are those who have difficulty creating visual pictures, and for them guided visualizations are not effective. If you are one of these people, don’t worry. Simply use the other tools, such as affirmations, to help you find your way into the Higher Self. I do suggest, however, that you give visualizations a try.
有些人在创作视觉图片时遇到了困难，对他们来说，引导性的视觉化并不是有效的。如果你是这些人中的一员，不要担心。简单地使用其他工具，比如自我肯定，来帮助你找到进入更高自我的方法。然而，我确实建议你尝试一下可视化。

It can be very effective to read aloud the visualizations given in books into your tape recorder, using as soothing a voice as you can project. By listening to visualization tapes by others, you will get a sense of the timing required between sentences to produce the full effect.
它可以是非常有效的朗读在你的磁带录音机里的图书给出的想象，使用尽可能舒缓的声音，你可以投射。通过聆听其他人的可视化磁带，你会感觉到句子之间所需的时间来产生完整的效果。

The following is a shortened version of a guided visualization I use in my classes and on my audio “The Art of Fearbusting.”
下面是我在课堂上和音频“ The Art of Fearbusting”中使用的一个指导性可视化的简短版本

Sit in a comfortable chair, with back straight, feet on the ground, hands comfortably on your lap. You have nothing to do but listen to my instructions and let whatever comes up for you come up. There is no right or wrong way to do a visualization. Just accept whatever comes up for you.
坐在舒适的椅子上，背部挺直，双脚着地，双手舒适地放在膝盖上。你什么也不用做，只要听从我的指示，让你遇到的任何事情都上来。想象没有对错之分。只要接受你遇到的任何事情。

Now close your eyes . . . and keep them closed throughout the visualization. Take a deep breath . . . inhale all the loving energy in the universe . . . and exhale all your loving energy into the universe . . . .Once more . . . in . . . and out . . . And once more . . . in . . . and . . . out. Feel how good it feels to begin to totally relax. Begin at the top of your head and work your way down to your feet . . . relax. Let go of the muscles . . . between your eyes . . . in your cheeks . . . mouth . . . neck . . . shoulders . . . back . . . arms . . . hands . . . chest . . . stomach . . . buttocks . . . legs . . . feet . . . .
现在闭上你的眼睛... 并且在整个想象过程中保持闭眼状态。深呼吸，吸入宇宙中所有的爱的能量，呼出你所有的爱的能量，再一次，吸入，呼出，再一次，吸入，呼出。感受开始完全放松的感觉有多好。从头顶开始，一直到脚。放松。放开两眼之间的肌肉，脸颊，嘴，脖子，肩膀，后背，胳膊，手，胸，腹，臀，腿，脚，脚。

Just totally let go . . . checking out any part of your body that may still be holding tension . . . and release it . . . .
完全放手，检查身体任何可能仍然保持紧张的部位，然后放手。

Now I want you to think of a goal that you have in life . . . a specific goal . . . and you know that FEAR is keeping you from moving forward toward that goal . . . .
现在我希望你们想一个你们人生中的目标，一个具体的目标，你们知道恐惧阻止你们朝着那个目标前进。

Now what I’d like you to do is to imagine yourself approaching that goal “as if” you had no fear . . . .
现在我想让你做的是想象你自己接近那个目标“好像”你没有恐惧。

I want you to see yourself approaching that goal with a sense of power and confidence in yourself . . . confidence that it will all be all right . . . .
我希望你看到自己带着力量和自信接近目标，相信一切都会好起来的。

What would you be doing . . . if you had no fear? . . .
如果你没有恐惧，你会做什么。

See yourself . . . . What would you be doing next . . . if you had no fear? . . .
看看你自己。如果你没有恐惧，你下一步会做什么？. . .

Look at the people around you . . . . How are you relating to them . . . with no fear? . . .
看看你周围的人。你是怎么和他们相处的，无所畏惧吗？. . .

How are they relating to you? . . .
他们和你有什么关系。

Just enjoy this sense of power and notice your ability to love . . . and contribute . . . .
只要享受这种力量的感觉，注意你的爱和贡献的能力。

And know that this is a feeling always within you . . . always a part of you . . . .
要知道这是一种永远在你心中的感觉，永远是你的一部分。

And it is within your capability to move forward in life with that power and with that confidence . . . .
这是在你的能力范围内，以这种力量和信心在生活中前进。

See yourself . . . actualizing your goal . . . with your power . . . with your confidence . . . with your love . . . and with your contribution . . . .
看看你自己，实现你的目标，用你的力量，用你的信心，用你的爱，用你的贡献。

And slowly . . . start to bring yourself back to this room . . . knowing that that power is available to you . . . . As soon as you begin to act . . . the power will come forward . . . .
慢慢地... 开始让自己回到这个房间... 知道那种力量是可以给你的..。一旦你开始行动，力量就会出现。

Feel yourself in your chair . . . be present in this room . . . listen to the sounds around you . . . and when you are ready, open your eyes . . . no need to rush. When you are ready, open your eyes.
感受你自己坐在椅子上的感觉。。。在这个房间里。。。聆听你周围的声音。。。当你准备好了的时候，睁开你的眼睛。。。。不要着急。当你准备好了，睁开你的眼睛。

Stretch and just feel the deliciousness of your power. It’s all there for the taking.
伸展一下，感受一下你力量的美妙，它们都在那里等着你去获取。

I suggest you or a friend with a soothing voice speak this visualization into a tape recorder, leaving ample space between each instruction to allow your imagination to create detail and feeling in the various scenes.
我建议你或者一个声音柔和的朋友用录音机把这种想象说出来，在每个指令之间留出足够的空间，让你的想象力在不同的场景中创造细节和感觉。

For those in my classes who tune in to visualizations, this one is very powerful. Many reported that for the first time they were able to see what the world looks like without fear. They reported that when they took away the fear, they were left with an abundance of love. They were startled by how beautiful the world appeared and how much they wanted to give to the people around them. If you have no concept of how the world can look without fear, it is hard to know what you are striving for. Once that vision is revealed to you, the Path is much easier to follow. You know when you are tuning in to the Higher Self and when you aren’t.
对于那些在我的课堂上收听可视化的人来说，这个是非常强大的。许多人报告说，他们第一次能够毫无恐惧地看到世界是什么样子。他们说，当他们带走了恐惧，他们留下了丰富的爱。他们惊讶于这个世界是多么的美丽，他们是多么的想要给予周围的人。如果你不知道这个世界如何看起来没有恐惧，你就很难知道你在为什么而奋斗。一旦这个愿景展现在你面前，这条道路就会变得更加容易遵循。你知道什么时候你正在调谐到更高的自我，什么时候你不是。

Visualizations can also be used to find answers to questions about the meaning and purpose of your life, or to clarify your life goals, or to reveal important truths that you hide from yourself. They have a multitude of applications, and offer incredible insights—so much so that they are valuable tools used by educators and therapists.
视觉化也可以用来找到关于你生活的意义和目的的问题的答案，或者澄清你的生活目标，或者揭示你对自己隐藏的重要真相。它们有大量的应用，并提供令人难以置信的洞察力——以至于它们是教育家和治疗师使用的有价值的工具。

I’ve presented you with a lot of ideas and a lot of tools, but to have them work effectively requires your trust. I’ve often been asked to prove that all I teach is true. I can only answer that certain things are not provable—at least, not yet.
我已经给你们提供了很多想法和工具，但是让它们有效工作需要你们的信任。我经常被要求证明我所教授的都是正确的。我只能回答，有些事情是无法证明的——至少现在还不能。

I can’t prove that a Higher Self exists. I can’t prove that we are connected to a wonderful source of healing, nurturing energy. I can’t prove that the Subconscious Mind can create “miracles” in both the inside and outside world. I can’t prove that the tools I’ve given you work. But I do know that when I use these concepts as the model for my life, my experiences are transformed and I find myself absolutely in love with life and everything that it entails—all of it. I can’t prove that I am right . . . nor can anyone prove that I am wrong. As Hugh Prather wrote in There Is a Place Where You Are Not Alone:
我不能证明更高的自我存在。我无法证明我们与一种奇妙的治愈、滋养能量的源泉相连。我无法证明潜意识可以在内部和外部世界创造“奇迹”。我无法证明我给你的工具是有效的。但我知道，当我用这些概念作为我生活的模型时，我的经历发生了转变，我发现自己完全热爱生活以及它所包含的一切——所有的一切。我不能证明我是对的... ... 也没有人能证明我是错的。正如休 · 普拉瑟在《有一个地方，你并不孤单》中写道:

WHY CHOOSE TO BE RIGHT INSTEAD OF HAPPY
为什么选择正确而不是快乐

WHEN THERE IS NO WAY TO BE RIGHT?
当没有办法是正确的时候？

Having experienced life from the vantage point of both the Chatterbox and the Higher Self, my choice is the latter. I will do whatever it takes to open myself up more and more to a mind and heart filled with love, joy, creativity, satisfaction, and peace. That is my goal and, having used the techniques in this book, I have traveled many miles toward that goal. I look forward to the many more I intend to go. I trust we will meet along the way . . . if we haven’t met already.
从话匣子和更高自我的有利位置体验生活后，我的选择是后者。我会不惜一切代价打开自己的心扉，让自己拥有一颗充满爱、喜悦、创造力、满足和平和的心。这就是我的目标，在使用了本书中的技巧之后，我已经朝着这个目标前进了许多英里。我期待着更多我打算去做的事情。我相信我们会在路上见面的，如果我们还没有见过面的话。
