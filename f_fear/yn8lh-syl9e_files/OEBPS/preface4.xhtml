Foreword to the 20th-Anniversary Edition
20周年纪念版前言

I do believe in miracles . . . and the success of Feel the Fear . . . and Do It Anyway is a miracle to me. So many publishers rejected it in the beginning that I almost gave up trying to have it published. After all, if you received a rejection letter, as I did, that said, “Lady Di could be cycling nude down the street giving this book away, nobody would read it,” wouldn’t you be tempted to give up trying?
我相信奇迹，感受恐惧的成功，无论如何去做，对我来说都是奇迹。一开始很多出版商都拒绝出版，以至于我几乎放弃了出版这本书的尝试。毕竟，如果你像我一样收到一封拒绝信，上面写着“我可以裸体骑自行车到街上把这本书送人，没有人会去读它”，你难道不会想放弃尝试吗？

I actually did put the manuscript away in a drawer for a few years and almost forgot about it. One day, I decided to clean out that particular drawer . . . and there it was . . . waiting for me. I sat down and read through it again. I couldn’t push away the feeling that my book had something important to say. This time I made a vow to myself: “Somehow I am going to find a way to get Feel the Fear . . . and Do It Anyway out into the world!” And so I “felt the fear” and tried once again to find a publisher. And this time, with a deep inner resolve and with the help of my agent, Dominick Abel, I found a publisher at last.
我确实把手稿放在抽屉里放了几年，差点忘了。有一天，我决定清理那个抽屉，它就在那里，等着我。我坐下，重新读了一遍。我无法摆脱这种感觉，我的书有重要的东西要说。这一次，我对自己发誓: “不管怎样，我要找到一种方法去感受恐惧... ... 并且无论如何都要在这个世界上去做!”于是我“感受到了恐惧”，并再次尝试寻找出版商。这一次，带着内心深处的决心，在我的经纪人多米尼克 · 阿贝尔(Dominick Abel)的帮助下，我终于找到了一个出版商。

Twenty years have passed since Feel the Fear . . . and Do It Anyway was actually published. It is now in more than one hundred countries and has been translated into more than thirty languages. And that number continues to grow. From this continuous growth, one can conclude that the words I wrote so long ago are as relevant today as they were then . . . if not more so!
《感受恐惧，无论如何去做》实际上已经出版20年了。它现在已经在100多个国家出版，并被翻译成30多种语言。而且这个数字还在继续增长。从这种持续的增长中，我们可以得出这样的结论: 我很久以前写的那些文字今天仍然和当时一样重要！

Fear will always be a part of all of our lives in one form or another. And this applies to people everywhere. From the letters of thanks I receive from all over the world, it is clear that no matter who we are, no matter where we live, no matter what we are experiencing in life, we all feel fear, but fear doesn’t need to hold us back from living a rich and beautiful life. We can overcome our fears by regularly using the powerful tools that lie within these pages.
恐惧总是以这样或那样的形式成为我们生活的一部分。这适用于任何地方的人。从我收到的来自世界各地的感谢信来看，很明显，无论我们是谁，无论我们生活在哪里，无论我们在生活中经历了什么，我们都会感到恐惧，但恐惧并不需要阻止我们过上丰富而美好的生活。我们可以通过定期使用这些页面中的强大工具来克服我们的恐惧。

I thank those who have communicated with me for generously letting me know that Feel the Fear . . . and Do It Anyway has made a huge difference in the quality of your lives. It brings me a special feeling of joy and humility to know that I have touched the lives of so many people.
我感谢那些与我交流的人，他们慷慨地让我知道，感受恐惧，并且无论如何都要去做，这对你们的生活质量产生了巨大的影响。当我知道我已经触动了那么多人的生活时，我感到特别的快乐和谦卑。

And for you “beginners” who are opening this book for the first time, trust me when I tell you that fear doesn’t have to hold you back. You, too, can learn how to push through your fears—all of your fears—with a sense of power, excitement, and love. And that’s what this book is all about.
对于你们这些第一次打开这本书的“初学者”来说，相信我，恐惧不一定会阻碍你们。你们也可以学习如何用力量、兴奋和爱的感觉克服你的恐惧——所有的恐惧。这就是这本书的内容。

As you will discover, the concepts and tools you are about to learn are meant to be used for a lifetime. Learn them well. And every time a new challenge comes into your life, you need only reach into your “toolbox” and move yourself from the weakest to the strongest part of who you are. As you do so, you will be absolutely amazed at the incredible amount of power you hold inside: power to love, power to succeed, power to help the world in your own special way. The journey to the best of who you are is exciting and rewarding, and I am so happy to be walking right along with you as you take those first important steps.
正如你们将会发现的，你们将要学习的概念和工具意味着要用一辈子。好好学习它们。每当一个新的挑战进入你的生活，你只需要伸出你的“工具箱”，让自己从最弱的部分变成最强的部分。当你这样做的时候，你会绝对惊讶于你内心拥有的难以置信的力量: 爱的力量，成功的力量，以你自己特殊的方式帮助世界的力量。成为最好的自己的旅程是令人兴奋和有益的，我很高兴在你们迈出重要的第一步的时候与你们同行。

From my heart to yours,
从我的心到你的心,
